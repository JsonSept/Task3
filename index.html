What is an OTAKU ?


**Otaku** is a Japanese term that describes people with consuming interests, particularly in anime, manga, video games, or computers ¹. The word's contemporary use originated with a 1983 essay by Akio Nakamori in Manga Burikko ¹. In Japan, the term is often used to refer to young people who are obsessed with technology or some aspect of pop culture and have poor social skills ¹. The word has since been adopted by other cultures and is now used more broadly to describe anyone with an intense or obsessive interest in a particular subject ².
